The data contains joint state information for the two exoskeletons, marked A and B,
across three 10-minute sessions of over-ground walking. The three-session splits are
noted by their corresponding row numbers (excluding the header) in the CSV file as
follows:
{
episode_stats:
start:
- 164041
- 454139
- 696306
end:
- 367374
- 654139
- 894640
}
